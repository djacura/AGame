Frequently Asked Questions?

Subscribing to AGame will give you access to a growing catalog of games, including any new releases that come out and also some Exclusives.

You can subscribe to AGame using a credit card, if this payment method is available in your country. And once Signed-up and Logged in you can click the Subscriptions link at the top of the Page.

If you decide to cancel your subscription, You can cancel by heading to Your profile page and clicking on the cancel subscription button. You can cancel at any time, without any need for notice. After this, the subscription will be cancelled and will not renew itself.

You can Edit your profile from the profile link at the top of the page, Click on My account, My Profile and then you can see your Profile Admin section, Your Delivery details, and also your order history.
